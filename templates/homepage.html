About us

Outbox, founded by a team of developers and sustainability enthusiasts, manages an eco-conscious thrift clothing store (Outbox) and an accompanying café (Beanbox). Initially driven by a mission to promote sustainability and increase revenue, the company's evolution is marked by the digitalization of its thrift store into an online e-commerce platform. ZES³, Outbox's online application, caters to fashion enthusiasts with affordable and vintage clothing, prioritizing eco-friendly practices. The integration of a physical café exemplifies Outbox's inventive approach, creating a unique and environmentally conscious shopping experience.
